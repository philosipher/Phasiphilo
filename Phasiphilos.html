Ghost Behaviors & Tests

Banshee

Unique scream on parabolic; targets one player. Test: Ignore non-target in hunt.

Demon

Early hunts; crucifix 5m radius. Test: Hunt above 50% sanity.

Deogen

Senses hidden players; heavy breathing on spirit box. Test: Always finds you.

Goryo

Stays in one room; no wandering. Test: No room changes.

Hantu

Visible breath; faster in cold; never turns breaker on. Test: Icy breath in hunts.

Jinn

25% sanity drop; fast with breaker on. Test: Sudden sanity loss.

Mare

Attacks in dark; turns off lights. Test: Hunt threshold changes with light.

Mimic

Copies others; extra orbs. Test: Inconsistent behaviors/evidence.

Moroi

Faster at low sanity; curses via spirit box. Test: Sanity pill speed check.

Myling

Quiet footsteps/voices far away. Test: Sounds fade quickly.

Obake

Unique fingerprints; shape-shifts. Test: 6-finger prints or no prints.

Oni

More active with people; no airball events. Test: Increased activity in groups.

Onryo

Blows out flames; hunts after 3rd. Test: Candle blow-out hunts.

Phantom

Disappears in photos; long blink. Test: Photo makes it vanish.

Poltergeist

Throws multiple objects. Test: Mass throws; EMF2 on ability.

Raiju

Fast near electronics. Test: Speed boost with active gear.

Revenant

Slow undetected, fast detected. Test: Incense hides to slow it.

Shade

Shy; low activity with people. Test: No events in room with players.

Spirit

No unique; longer smudge no-hunt. Test: 180s post-smudge safe.

Thaye

Ages over time; slows/weakens. Test: Early fast hunts, later slow.

Twins

Dual interactions; decoy slower. Test: Two EMF from different spots.

Wraith

No salt footprints; teleports. Test: Salt walk no UV.

Yokai

Hunts on voice; limited hearing. Test: Talk to trigger hunt.

Yurei

Drains sanity fast; doors shut. Test: Smudge confines it.
