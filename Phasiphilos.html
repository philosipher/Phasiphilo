Banshee

Expanded Behaviors: Fixates on one random player (the "target") for the entire contract, checking only their sanity for hunts (ignores team average). Roams toward the target even outside the ghost room if on the same floor. Performs singing ghost events more often (up to 50% chance vs. 10-15% for others). During hunts, ignores non-target players completely, even if they're closer or in LoS‚Äîwill walk past them to chase the target.
Unique Traits: Unique ultrasonic scream audible only on parabolic mic (up to 33% chance per use, 100% ID if heard). Weak to crucifixes (prevents hunts within 5m vs. 3m normal). Chronicle update: Can now fully change ghost room to follow target (previously limited).
Strengths: Relentless targeting makes hunts deadly for one player; high sanity target delays hunts team-wide.
Weaknesses: If target hides or stays high sanity (>50%), no hunts; ignores others, allowing safe evidence gathering.
Hunt Patterns: Starts at 50% of target's sanity; standard 1.7 m/s speed, but may roam far to initiate near target. Grace period: 3s (normal).
Interactions: Frequent interactions near target (e.g., doors, objects); singing events drain 15% sanity if heard.
Advanced Tests: In multiplayer, bait a hunt and have non-target players stand in open with incense‚ÄîBanshee ignores them completely, walking past to chase the target. Sanity test: Hunts at 50% of target's sanity only‚Äîmonitor individual sanity via pills; if hunts when team average >50% but one low, likely Banshee.
